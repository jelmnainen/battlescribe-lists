Force Rules

Foresight of the Damned (Ulthwe): Roll a dice each time a model in your kill team loses a wound. On a 6, the damage is ignored and the model does not lose a wound. If a model already has a similar ability, choose which effect applies, and re-roll 1s when making these rolls. ()
